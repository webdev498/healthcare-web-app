Billing Policies

Updating Credit Card

Patients may update and save credit card information at any time. Changes will become affective on the next scheduled billing date.

Subscription Plan Cancellation

Patients may opt to cancel their individual or family subscription plan at any time and it will take effect at the end of their biling cycle. Partial-month refunds will not be issued.

Subscription Plan Upgrade

Patients can transition from an individual plan to a family plan at any time and access for new members becomes affective immediately. The difference is billed at the time of the change. The updated balance will be charged on the existing monthly biling schedule.

Subscription Plan Downgrade

Patients can transition from an family plan to an individual plan at any time. Family plan access remains active until the start of the next biling schedule. The updated balance will be charged on the existing monthly biling schedule. Partial-month refunds will not be issued.

Adding Additional Family Member(s)

Additional family members may be added to a full family account effective immediately. Additional member charges will be billed at the time of the charge. The updated balance will be charged on the existing monthly biling schedule.
