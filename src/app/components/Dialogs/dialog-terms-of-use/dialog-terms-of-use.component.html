Subscription and Registration for Texas Residents

Subscription access is available for Texas residents only. All users must reside in Texas a minimum of 6 months out of the calendar year.

eMD Access is not an insurance plan. eMD Access is a subscription web-based service that enables direct access to physicians.

Subscription is automatically renewed monthly, or every 30 days. At the end of the monthly subscription period, you will automatically be signed up and billed for an additional subscription term of 30 days. If you do not wish your subscription to auto-renew, you may remove your credit card information prior to the end of your current subscription term.
