Patients may update and save credit card information at any time. Changes will become effective on the next schedule billing date.

Subscription Plan Cancellation

Patients can opt to cancel their individual or family subscription plan at any time and it will take effect at the end of their billing cycle. Partial-month refunds will not be issued.

Subscription Plan Updgrade

Patients can transition from an individual plan to a familly plan at any time and access for new members become effective immediately. The difference is billed at the time of the change. The updated balance will be charge on the existing monthlybilling schedule.

Subscription Plan Downgrade

Patients can transition from family plan to an individual plan at any time. Family plan acces remains active until the start of the next billing cycle. The updated balance will be charge on the existing monthly billing schedule. Partial-month refunds will not be issued.

Adding Additional Family Member(s)

Additional family members may be added to a full family account effective immediately. Additional member charges will be billed at the time of the charge. The updated balance will be charged on the existing monthly billing schedule.
